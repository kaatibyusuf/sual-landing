Disciplines

The seven Islamic sciences

Each discipline on Sual is treated with the depth it deserves — a serious introduction to the classical tradition, structured across Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced levels.

01
FiqhالفِقْهIslamic jurisprudence

Fiqh is the science of deriving practical rulings from the sources of Islamic law — covering worship, transactions, family law, and everyday conduct. You'll work through core rulings on salah, zakah, sawm, and hajj at the beginner level, then move into the evidences and scholarly positions behind classical fiqh questions.

02
SeerahالسِّيرَةProphetic biography

Seerah is the study of the life of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ — his character, his struggles, his companions, and the lessons drawn from his biography. Sual covers the major events of his life chronologically with attention to the wisdom behind each episode.

03
ArabiyyahالعَرَبِيَّةArabic language

Arabiyyah is the gateway to every other Islamic science — the language in which the Quran, hadith, and the entire classical tradition were written. Coverage spans vocabulary, sentence structure, and reading comprehension, building toward independent engagement with classical texts.

Beginner — liveIntermediate — liveAdvanced — live
Study Arabiyyah →
04
Usul al-Fiqhأُصُولُ الفِقْهPrinciples of jurisprudence

Usul al-Fiqh is the methodology behind fiqh — how scholars derive rulings from the Quran, Sunnah, ijma, and qiyas. Essential for understanding why scholars differ and how rulings are actually reached.

Beginner — liveIntermediate — liveAdvanced — live
Study Usul al-Fiqh →
05
SarfالصَّرْفArabic morphology

Sarf is the study of word formation in Arabic — how a single root branches into dozens of related words through patterns of derivation. Unlocks the internal logic of the Arabic lexicon.

Beginner — liveIntermediate — liveAdvanced — live
Study Sarf →
06
NahwالنَّحْوArabic syntax

Nahw is Arabic grammar — the rules governing how words relate to one another within a sentence, including case endings and sentence structure. Mastery allows correct parsing of any Quranic verse or classical text.

Beginner — liveIntermediate — liveAdvanced — live
Study Nahw →
07
TafseerالتَّفْسِيرQuranic exegesis

Tafseer is the science of explaining the Quran — its meanings, context of revelation, linguistic nuances, and the rulings and lessons derived from each verse, drawing on classical tafseer works.
