Why Sual

Built on classical scholarship

Sual is not another generic Islamic app. Every piece of content is grounded in the classical tradition — here's exactly why that matters.

📖
Primary source citations
Every answer on Sual cites its classical source — not a paraphrase of a paraphrase. You can trace every ruling, narration, or linguistic point back to where it actually comes from.
🎓
Scholarly voice
Content is written at the level of a serious student of knowledge — not simplified to the point of distortion, and not so dense it becomes inaccessible. The goal is depth you can actually use.
✍️
Arabic-first
Arabic terminology is preserved throughout with full tashkeel, proper transliteration following sun and moon letter rules, and linguistic analysis where it adds real understanding.
🕋
Seven sciences together
No other free app covers Fiqh, Seerah, Arabiyyah, Usul al-Fiqh, Sarf, Nahw, and Tafseer at this depth across three structured learning levels — all in one place.
🤲
Free at its core
No ads. No paywalls on the knowledge content. Spaces is paid because running a real community costs real resources — but the app itself is free and always will be.
📴
Works offline
Install Sual and study without an internet connection. Built for the student in every circumstance — on the train, in low signal, or anywhere else.
