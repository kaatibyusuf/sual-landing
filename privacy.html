Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Sual ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Sual mobile and web application (the "Service"), an Islamic knowledge platform. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Information we collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Profile photo, if you choose to upload one
  • Quiz results and learning progress data
  • Posts and replies you submit within the Spaces community feature

If you use our prayer times feature, we may request access to your approximate location to calculate accurate prayer timings. This data is used only for this purpose and is not stored on our servers.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Track your learning progress and quiz history
  • Send you account-related emails, such as confirmation, password reset, and welcome messages
  • Operate the Spaces community feature for paid subscribers
  • Improve and personalize your experience

Payment information

Subscriptions to Spaces are processed through Paystack, a third-party payment processor. We do not store your card details. Please refer to Paystack's privacy policy for information on how they handle payment data.

Data storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, our backend infrastructure provider. We implement industry-standard security measures, including row-level security policies, to protect your information.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your information with third parties except as necessary to provide the Service (such as our payment processor, email provider, and hosting infrastructure) or as required by law.

Your rights

You may:

  • Access and update your profile information at any time within the app
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us
  • Opt out of non-essential emails

Children's privacy

Our Service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at noreply@usesual.com.
