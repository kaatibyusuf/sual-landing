✍️ Arabiyyah class فَصْلُ العَرَبِيَّة

A complete structured Arabic language programme from absolute beginner to mastery of the classical sciences. Three levels, each building directly on the last, with the goal of independent reading of unvowelled classical Arabic texts.

Beginnerمُبْتَدِئ
Duruusu-l-Lughah Al-Arabiyyah
دُرُوسُ اللُّغَةِ العَرَبِيَّة

Complete study of the three volumes of Duruus Al-Lughah Al-Arabiyyah by Dr V. Abdur Raheem. Covers reading, writing, vocabulary, basic grammar, and conversation with weekly tests. Duration: 6 months, 5 hours per week.

Intermediateمُتَوَسِّط
Nahw, Sarf, and Insha
النَّحْو وَالصَّرْف وَالإِنْشَاء

Systematic study of grammar through Al-Ajrumiyyah and Qatr An-Nada, morphology through Shudha Al-Urf, and guided composition. Duration: 8 months, 7 hours per week.

Advancedمُتَقَدِّم
Classical sciences — Alfiyyah, Sarf, Balaghah
الأَلْفِيَّة وَالصَّرْف وَالبَلَاغَة

Study at the level of the traditional Madrasah curriculum — Alfiyyah Ibn Malik, advanced Sarf, and Balaghah. Duration: 12 months, 10 hours per week.

📜 Hadeeth class فَصْلُ الحَدِيث

A structured Hadeeth memorization and study programme — from the Forty of An-Nawawi to the great collections of the Sunnah, building toward serious engagement with the science of hadeeth.

Beginnerمُبْتَدِئ
Al-Arba'oon An-Nawawiyyah
الأَرْبَعُونَ النَّوَوِيَّة

Complete memorization and study of the Forty Hadith of Imam An-Nawawi, with chains, commentary, and daily application. Duration: 6 months, 3 hours per week.

Intermediateمُتَوَسِّط
Bulugh Al-Maram and Umdat Al-Ahkam
بُلُوغُ المَرَام وَعُمْدَةُ الأَحْكَام

Memorization and study of the two greatest legal hadith collections, covering 414 hadith on worship and transactions. Duration: 10 months, 6 hours per week.

Advancedمُتَقَدِّم
Sahih Bukhari memorization
حِفْظُ صَحِيح البُخَارِي

Memorization of selected hadith from Sahih Al-Bukhari alongside Fath Al-Bari, plus hadith sciences. Duration: 18 months, 12 hours per week.
