✍️ Arabiyyah Class فَصْلُ العَرَبِيَّة

A complete structured Arabic language programme from absolute beginner to mastery of the classical sciences. Three levels, each building directly on the last, with the goal of independent reading of unvowelled classical Arabic texts.

🌱 Beginner مُبْتَدِئ
Duruusu-l-Lughah Al-Arabiyyah
دُرُوسُ اللُّغَةِ العَرَبِيَّة

Complete study of the three volumes of Duruus Al-Lughah Al-Arabiyyah by Dr V. Abdur Raheem — the gold standard for learning Arabic from scratch. Covers reading, writing, vocabulary, basic grammar, and simple conversation, with weekly vocabulary and dictation tests. Duration: 6 months, 5 hours per week. Outcome: read Arabic text with vowels fluently and write simple Arabic sentences.

📖 Intermediate مُتَوَسِّط
Nahw, Sarf, and Insha
النَّحْو وَالصَّرْف وَالإِنْشَاء

Systematic study of Arabic grammar (Nahw) through Al-Ajrumiyyah and Qatr An-Nada, morphology (Sarf) through Shudha Al-Urf, and guided Arabic composition (Insha). Includes weekly i'rab exercises on Quranic verses. Duration: 8 months, 7 hours per week. Outcome: perform full i'rab of Quranic verses and write correct Arabic prose.

🏛️ Advanced مُتَقَدِّم
Classical Sciences — Alfiyyah, Sarf, Balaghah
الأَلْفِيَّة وَالصَّرْف وَالبَلَاغَة

Study at the level of the traditional Madrasah curriculum — Alfiyyah Ibn Malik with the commentary of Ibn Aqil, advanced Sarf through Maqsud and Al-Kaylani, and Balaghah through Al-Balaghatul-Wadihah. Duration: 12 months, 10 hours per week. Outcome: read unvowelled classical Arabic texts independently.

📜 Hadeeth Class فَصْلُ الحَدِيث

A structured Hadeeth memorization and study programme — from the Forty of An-Nawawi to the great collections of the Sunnah, building toward serious engagement with the science of hadeeth.

🌱 Beginner مُبْتَدِئ
Al-Arba'oon An-Nawawiyyah
الأَرْبَعُونَ النَّوَوِيَّة

Complete memorization and study of the Forty Hadith of Imam An-Nawawi — the foundational text of Islamic learning for over seven centuries. Every hadith is memorized in Arabic with its chain, studied for its meanings via An-Nawawi's commentary, and applied to daily life. Duration: 6 months, 3 hours per week. Outcome: memorize all 42 hadith and extract basic Islamic rulings from them.

📖 Intermediate مُتَوَسِّط
Bulugh Al-Maram and Umdat Al-Ahkam
بُلُوغُ المَرَام وَعُمْدَةُ الأَحْكَام

Memorization and study of the two greatest collections of legal hadith — Bulugh Al-Maram by Ibn Hajar Al-Asqalani and Umdatul-Ahkam by Ibn Qudamah, covering 414 hadith on acts of worship and transactions. Duration: 10 months, 6 hours per week. Outcome: memorize core legal hadith and understand how scholars derive fiqh rulings from hadith.

🏛️ Advanced مُتَقَدِّم
Sahih Bukhari Memorization
حِفْظُ صَحِيح البُخَارِي

The pinnacle of hadith study — systematic memorization and deep study of Sahih Al-Bukhari, the most authentic book after the Quran, alongside Fath Al-Bari, Ibn Hajar's great commentary. Includes hadith sciences and independent research assignments. Duration: 18 months, 12 hours per week. Outcome: memorize 200+ hadith and engage with hadith sciences at an advanced level.
