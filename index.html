Features

Everything you need to study Islam

Built for the serious student who wants depth, not surface-level summaries.

📚
Levelled Scholarly Q&A
700+ curated questions and detailed answers across three levels; Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced. Sourced from classical texts and authenticated references.
🎯
Levelled Quiz Mode
Test your knowledge per discipline or in mixed format, filtered by your level. Scores are saved and tracked to measure your progress over time.
🤲
Adhkar & Duas
Complete morning and evening adhkar, ruqyah verses with methodology, and a library of authenticated duas, all with Arabic text, transliteration, translation, and scholarly sources.
📜
Stories of the Salaf
Detailed biographical narratives of the Companions and Successors with primary source citations and lessons for modern Muslim life.
🗓️
Islamic Calendar
A full Hijri calendar with important Islamic dates; Ayyamul-Bid, Ashura, Eid, Laylatul-Qadr, with location-based Maghrib adjustment for accurate date transitions.
🎙️
Tajweed Science
Ten comprehensive sections covering Noon Saakinah, Madd, Qalqalah, Tafkheem, Waqf, Ghunnah, and more... with rules, examples, and Quranic illustrations.
🌐
Spaces Community
A paid members-only space for discussion, scholar Q&A, and structured classes in Arabiyyah and Hadeeth. The serious student's community.

Learning Levels

Three levels. One clear path.

Wherever you are in your journey, Sual meets you there and takes you further.

🌱 Beginner — مُبْتَدِئ
Foundations of the Faith
أُسُسُ الدِّين
Core rulings, essential fiqh, introductory seerah, basic Arabic grammar, and the fundamental principles every Muslim must know. Accessible, deep, and sourced.
📖 Intermediate — مُتَوَسِّط
Deeper into the Sciences
أَعْمَقُ فِي العُلُوم
Technical usul, detailed morphological and syntactic analysis, advanced seerah narrations, classical fiqh positions with their evidences, and Quranic linguistic depth.
🏛️ Advanced — مُتَقَدِّم
Classical Scholarship
العِلْمُ الكِلَاسِيكِيّ
Study at the level of classical Islamic scholarship — primary texts, scholarly commentary, advanced usul disputes, and the sciences that produce independent students of knowledge. Coming soon.

Why Sual

Built on classical scholarship

Sual is not another generic Islamic app. Every piece of content is grounded in the classical tradition.

01
Primary Source Citations
Every answer cites its classical source.
02
Scholarly Voice
Content written at the level of a serious student of knowledge, not simplified to the point of distortion, not inaccessible to the point of uselessness.

Arabic-First
Arabic terminology preserved throughout with full tashkeel, proper transliteration with sun and moon letter rules, and linguistic analysis where relevant.
