Features

Everything you need to study Islam

Built for the serious student who wants depth, not surface-level summaries.

📚
Scholarly Q&A
350+ curated questions and detailed answers across seven disciplines, sourced from classical texts and authenticated references.
🎯
Quiz Mode
Test your knowledge with 350+ questions per discipline or in a mixed format. Track your score and review your answers.
🃏
Flashcards
Six decks of Arabic and Islamic terminology flashcards with Arabic script and transliteration.
📜
Stories of the Salaf
Detailed biographical narratives of the Companions and Successors with primary source citations.
🤲
Dua Collection
30 authenticated duas from the Quran and Sunnah with Arabic text, transliteration, translation, and virtue.
🗓️
Islamic Calendar
A full Hijri calendar with important Islamic dates circled and explained — Ayyam al-Bid, Ashura, Eid, Laylat al-Qadr.
🎙️
Tajweed Science
Ten comprehensive sections covering Noon Saakinah, Madd, Qalqalah, Tafkheem, Waqf, Ghunnah and more.
🌙
Dark Mode & Accessibility
Full dark mode, adjustable font size, and a clean interface designed for extended study sessions.

Why Sual

Built on classical scholarship

Sual is not another generic Islamic app. Every piece of content is grounded in the classical tradition.

01
Primary Source Citations
Every answer cites its classical source — from Sahih al-Bukhari and Sahih Muslim to Ibn Hisham, al-Nawawi, Ibn Hajar, and al-Shatibi.
02
Scholarly Voice
Content written at the level of a serious student of knowledge — not simplified to the point of distortion.
03
Arabic-First
Arabic terminology preserved throughout — with full tashkeel, transliteration, and linguistic analysis.
04
Seven Sciences Together
No other free app covers Fiqh, Seerah, Arabiyyah, Usul al-Fiqh, Sarf, Nahw, and Tafseer at this depth.
05
Completely Free
No ads, no paywalls, no subscription. Knowledge of the Deen belongs to everyone.
06
Works Offline
Install it on your phone and study without an internet connection. Progress saved locally.
